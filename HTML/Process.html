Process

When it comes down to it, I'm essentially a curator.

When I decide to create something new, I grab my camera and go for a walk. Along the walk I keep a careful and observing eye out for different textures, objects, and forms. Whether it be the crack in the side-walk, the rust on the bottom of a car, or that particular tree in the park, if it catches my eye, I take photos of it. I like to think of it as a sort of treasure-hunt.

I shoot photos of objects along my walk, then bring those images into Photoshop. Once I'm there I manipulate the images to my liking, then start to collage the visual assets I just created.

Depending on the project I'm working on, I'll reference certain feelings, concepts, and imagery to give my work a particular direction.

I view what I do as curation, because I hand pick objects I see and blend them together with other objects in order to create new forms. I essentially 'sample' the world around me.

After you click off my website, I encourage you to take a look at your surroundings, and look for the simple beauty of the things that surround you. I'm sure you'll see something to your liking.

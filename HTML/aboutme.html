About Me

Throughout my life I've always enjoyed taking a step back and observing things.

Whether it be to solve a problem, self reflect, or to simply enjoy the present moment, I value tapping into life with an open eye. I like to believe that I'm a calm, rational person who holds great tenacity to achieve goals that hold meaning to me.

Working in teams is something that brings me great value. Being alongside others in pursuit of a common end is something familiar, and satisfying to me. This really came to light during my time at the University of Waterloo in the Global Business and Digital Arts program, where most of the deliverables set out for us involved long-term group collaboration.

My interests lay within digital art, clothing, music, anime, and select video games. I'm currently within the top 0.15% of North America in a game called Team Fight Tactics, a strategy driven auto-chess game. I quite enjoy this game due to it's option and time based nature. I have to be constantly engaged in order to traverse through the options presented to me, all the while factoring in the many variables existing within the game
